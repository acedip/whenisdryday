When is Dry Day

So what is Whenisdryday.in

A plain and simple email subscription site, away from the hassles of longin or account creation. Just put in your eMail Address and State and we will send you an email before the dry day in your state.
Now what is Dry Day? We'd rather refrain from commenting on stupid laws but Here is for the spirited ones.

About the people behind Whenisdryday.in

On one fine wintery saturday evening in Banagalore, a group of folks decided to go out and mingle. Eat, drink and whatever kids do these days. They were just about to venture out when suddenly someone amongst the fine gentlemen said "Its Dry Day you fools".
Nothing happened then.
